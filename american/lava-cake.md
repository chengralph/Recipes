# Chocolate Molten Lava Cakes
[https://www.melskitchencafe.com/my-favorite-chocolate-molten-lava-cakes/](https://www.melskitchencafe.com/my-favorite-chocolate-molten-lava-cakes/)

## Ingredients

### Core (Servings 6)

* 1 cup (170 g) semisweet or bittersweet chocolate
* 10 tbsp salted butter
* Pinch of salt (⅛ tsp; add more if using unsalted butter)
* ½ tsp vanilla extract
* 3 large eggs
* 3 large egg yolks
* 1 ½ cups (171 g) powdered sugar
* ½ cup (71 g) all-purpose flour

### For Ramekins

* 2–3 tbsp softened butter
* 2–3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

## Instructions

1. Oven @ **375–400°F** (use 375°F if your oven runs hot).
2. Grease ramekins well with butter → dust with cocoa → tap out excess.
3. Melt chocolate + butter until smooth. Stir in salt + vanilla. Cool slightly.
4. Beat eggs + yolks **2–3 min** until thick and pale.
5. Add powdered sugar → mix. Fold in chocolate mixture + flour just until combined.
6. Divide batter into 6 ramekins. Place on a baking sheet.
7. Bake **12–13 min**: edges set, centers soft.
8. Rest 2–3 min. Loosen edges and invert onto plates.
9. Serve with powdered sugar, whipped cream, berries, or ice cream.

### Notes

* Ghirardelli/Guittard chocolate melts best.
* Make-ahead: fill ramekins, cover, refrigerate 1–2 days. Add **30–60 sec** to baking time.
