# Sweet Tea / Lemon Tea

## Ingredients

### Core Sweet Tea (6 Liters)

* 6 L water
* 14 Lipton black tea bags 
* 500 g rock sugar
* 3 lemons


## Instructions

1. Bring **6** of water to a boil.
2. Turn off heat → add **14 tea bags** Add **500 g rock sugar** → cover and steep 1 hour
3. Add lemons with feshnet/strainer for 5min.
4. Chill in fridge **3–4 hours**.
