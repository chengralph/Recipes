# Clay Pot Chicken Rice

## Ingredients

### Core
- 300ml long grain rice (e.g., Thai jasmine)
- 1 link Chinese sausage
- 3 dried shiitake mushrooms 
- 1 bone-in chicken thigh or chicken thigh
- 1 stalk scallion

### Chicken Marinade
- 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1.5 tbsp water
- 1/2 tsp cooking wine
- 1/8 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp oil
- 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
- 1/2 shallot
- 1/2 ginger

### Mushroom Marinade

- 1/4 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch

### Serving Sauce or use Cassrole sauce
- 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/2 tsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce
- 1/2 tsp sugar

### Cooking
- 1/2 tsp oil
- 320ml boiling water
- 1 tbsp oil

## Instructions

1. Soak rice, sausage in boiling water for 10min. Mushrooms in boiling water for 20min.
2. Cut chicken, shallot, ginger, scallion; marinate chicken and mushrooms.
3. Mix serving sauce or use clay pot sauce.
4. Oil sides. Cook rice with oil and ~320ml boiling water in clay pot.
5. Add sausage, chicken, mushrooms; cook on low for 10min. 
6. Medium heat. Tilt for 2min. Another 2min for another side (4min total)
7. Low heat. Add oils on side and let it drip for 2min. (2min)
7. Garnish with scallion, add sauce, serve.
