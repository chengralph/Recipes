# Honey Chicken Wings

## Ingredients

### Core

- 10 - 12 wings
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp water

### Marinade

- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp soy suace
- 1/2 tsp salt
- chopped onion
- chopped ginger

## Instructions

1. Marinade wings
2. Mix water/honey
3. Wings on rack. Brush honey. Bake for 15@380F
4. Flip. Brush honey. Bake 20@380F
