# Korean Galbi

## Ingredients

### Core

- 1.5lb short rib

### Marinade

- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup rice wine
- 1/2 tspn of sesame oil

### Puree (Part of marinade)

- pinch of black pepper
- 1 medium onion
- 8 garlic cloves
- 1 asian pear (diced)
- 1/2 inch ginger

## Instructions

1. Purree items. Mix with mariande. Marinade ribs overnight.
2. Sear for 2-5min per side.
