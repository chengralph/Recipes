# Thai Iced Tea

## Ingredients

### Thai Tea Base
- ¾ cup Thai tea leaves
- 5 cups hot off-the-boil water
- ¼ cup sugar
- ⅛ tsp salt

### Per Glass
- ¾ cup Thai tea base
- 1½ tbsp sweetened condensed milk
- 2–3 tbsp evaporated milk
- 1 pint glass ice

## Instructions

1. Add Thai tea leaves to hot water and steep for 5min.
2. Strain tea leaves. Stir in sugar and salt while hot.
3. Let tea base cool or chill.
4. Fill a pint glass completely with ice.
5. Pour in ¾ cup Thai tea base.
6. Stir in sweetened condensed milk.
7. Top with evaporated milk and serve.
