# Soy Sauce Chicken Wings (Chinese Braised)

## Ingredients

### Core
- 18 chicken wings

### Aromatics
- 5 slices ginger
- 1/2 cup spring onion (cut into 2" pieces)
- 3 star anise
- 1/2 cinnamon stick
- 1 black cardamom (crushed)

### Sauce
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup light soy sauce
- 4 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/2 cup rock sugar

### For Blanching
- Water (enough to cover wings)
- Ginger (from above)
- Spring onion (from above)
- Ice bath

## Instructions

### Prep / Blanch
1. In a medium pot, add enough water to cover wings and bring to a boil.
2. Add ginger and spring onion. Cook for 30 seconds.
3. Add chicken wings.
4. When water returns to boil, cook for 3 minutes.
5. Remove wings and transfer to ice bath.
6. Let cool completely, then drain and dry.

### Braise
1. Clean the pot.
2. Add:
   - 1 cup water  
   - Light soy sauce  
   - Dark soy sauce  
   - Oyster sauce  
   - Rock sugar  
   - Star anise  
   - Cinnamon stick  
   - Black cardamom  
   - Chicken wings  

3. Bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to low simmer.
5. Cook 15–20 minutes with lid off.
6. Turn wings occasionally for even color.

### Finish
1. Let sauce reduce until thick, glossy, and coats wings.
2. If sauce is not thick enough:
   - Remove wings
   - Boil sauce on high 1–2 minutes
3. Pour sauce over wings.

Serve hot.
