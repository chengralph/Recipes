# Soy Suace wings

## Ingredients

### Core

- 4 qurter legs

### Aromatics

- 5-8 slices of ginger
- green onion
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 star anise

### Sauce

- 3 tablespoons (45ml) regular soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons (7.5ml) dark soy sauce
- 2 Tablespoon Oyster suace
- 2 tablespoon shaoxing wine
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of white pepper
- 2 star anise
- 500ml  water
- Rock sugar (28g)

### At the end

- 1 tablespoon of the sauce above
- 1 tablespoon of dark soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Sear ginger + aromatics
2. Boil. Put chicken in face down. 
3. Turn to medium heat for 15m. 
4. Soak for another 30m with no heat.
