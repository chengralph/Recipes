# Wuxi Ribs

## Ingredients

### Core
- 2lb of pork ribs
- Oil



### Aromatics
- 5 slices of ginger
- 3 scallions


### Sweetener
- 45g rock sugar
- 1 tablespoon honey


### Sauce
- 1/4 cup shaoxing
- 1/4 cup soy suace
- 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
- 1/4 cup chicken stock
- 1 star anisse
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 small cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Parboil ribs for 3-4minutes.
2. Sautee the ginger and scallion.
3. Add rock sugar and honey
4. Add ribs and sautee. 
5. Add sauce. 
6. High pressure for 15min + Quick release.
