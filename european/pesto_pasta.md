# Pesto Pasta

## Ingredients

### Core
- 2 services of pasta
- Salt/Pepper
- Olive Oil
- Chicken thigh 3
- Oregano


### Pesto
- 1 bundle parsley
- 1 bundle of basil
- 4-6 tablepsoon pine nuts (or pistatchios or walnuts)
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 6 cloves of garlic
- Pinch of salt
- .75 cup of parmesean
- 1/2 cup of left over pasta water (after the rest)
- 3 Dashes of heavy cream



## Instructions
1. Boil water for pasta.
2. Blend parsley, pune nuts, olive oil, garlic, salt, parmesean
4. Cook pasta with salt, oil per instructions
5. Dry and season chicken with salt, pepper oregano
6. Heat pan, and sear chicken 5min on each side.
7. Save some pasta water, and drain pasta when done. 
8. In same pot heat to medium, olive oil, then add pesto. Add heavy cream. 
9. Stir and season to taste. 
10. Rest chicken for 2min, put everyting together
