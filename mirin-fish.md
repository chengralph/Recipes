# Mirin Salmon/Cod

## Ingredients

### Core

- Salmon/Cod
- Bok choy

### Marinade

- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp dashi (Can skip. Or use sake)

### Optional

- Yuzu
- Miso Paste

## Instructions

1. Marinade Salmon/Cod
2. Place fish and vegetable in tray. Bake 350@15min
3. Remove vegetable. Broil for 2-7min.
